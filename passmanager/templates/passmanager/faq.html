Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How much does PassManager cost?

PassManager is completely free of charge. However, donations are appreciated to support our ongoing development efforts.

Is my user data safe with PassManager?

Yes, we prioritize your security. We never share your data with third parties. Your passwords are encrypted and hashed using state-of-the-art methods before being stored in our database.

How can I verify the security claims?

We believe in transparency. Our entire codebase is open-source and available on GitHub. Feel free to review it at any time.

Why hasn't PassManager upgraded to a custom domain or a better hosting plan?

PassManagerWeb is currently self-funded and focuses on enhancing user experience first. Upgrades to our hosting plan are planned before considering a custom domain, to ensure smooth service operation.

Why choose PassManager over larger competitors?

We are a dedicated team passionate about providing high-quality features without paywalls. Our focus is on user satisfaction and service excellence rather than profit or competition.

Why is PassManager sometimes slow?

PassManager is operated by a small team committed to providing a free service. While we strive for optimal performance, limitations in resources may occasionally affect speed.

Do you offer two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Yes, two-factor authentication (2FA) is required by default for enhanced security. This extra layer of protection ensures that even if your password is compromised, your account remains secure.

What happens if I forget my master password?

We do not store your master password, so we cannot recover it for you. Please ensure you keep your master password in a safe place. If you forget it, you will need to create a new account.

Do you have mobile apps available?

Currently, we do not offer dedicated mobile apps. However, you can create a shortcut to our web app on your mobile device's home screen for easy access.

Is there a limit to the number of passwords/accounts I can store?

No, there is no limit on the number of passwords or accounts you can store.

What level of customer support do you provide?

For customer support, please contact us via email.

Do you have a data breach history?

No, PassManager is a new tool, and we have not experienced any data breaches to date. We prioritize the security and privacy of our users. We hope to continue this record in the future.
